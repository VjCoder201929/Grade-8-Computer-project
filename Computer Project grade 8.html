Cyber Threats and Security

Computers and the internet have become an important part of our lives and are a nessecity in most spheres today.
Although networks of today have simplified the communication process and provided great opportunities in almost all spheres,there are various types of challenges and threats associated with the use of these networks today. They are known as cybercrimes.


CyberCrime

A cybercrime is any illegal activity done through the internet (email spoofing, identity theft, transmitting virus etc).
CyberCrimes can be divided into three major categories which are as follows:-

  • Crime against an individual person
  • Crime against property
  • Crime against an organisation/society

Cyber Security

Cyber security is a collection of technologies, processes and practises designed to protect networks, computers, programs and data from attack, damage or unauthorised access.
